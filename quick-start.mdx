### Prerequistes
- A model

If you don't have an *.GLB extension model, make sure to convert to GLB in Blender.

Also if you don't know how to convert your minecraft map into model, follow the [Mineways Tutorial](/using-mineways) we have. Blockbench users can follow by [clicking here](/using-blockbench).
